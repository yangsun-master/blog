LEGO Tree House

Last updated: 2019-12-01

This is my favorite LEGO set: Tree House. The tree house is amazing, there’re so many details. When I play with it, and I can imagine I’m living in it. I found one drawback the room was dark when the tree has top leaves. hahaha. The real tree’s leaves make a shadow, too. How can I complain about this? But I have my own idea. I provide the electricity to the tree house, and light up the tree house. It’s so beautiful.

Behind the scene, this set belongs to Ideas collection. The Ideas collection is very special, they’re created by LEGO fans, not the LEGO company. Here’s the short version of the story, the super LEGO fans made the tree house, and submit to LEGO. Once he got 10K supporters from the other LEGO fans, the LEGO made this as official LEGO product. Awesome!
